Goa


Goa is one of my favorite holiday destinations in India. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese architecture, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach - A popular beach with exciting water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada - A historic Portuguese fort with scenic views.
  3. Dudhsagar Falls - A magnificent four-tiered waterfall.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus - A famous UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  5. Anjuna Beach - Known for its flea markets and nightlife.
