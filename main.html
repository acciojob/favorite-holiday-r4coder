TIRUPATI

Tirupati is a renowned pilgrimage city in the state of Andhra Pradesh. It is famous for the sacred Sri Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited and richest Hindu temples in the world. Located at the foothills of the Seshachalam Hills, Tirupati attracts millions of devotees every year who come to seek the blessings of Lord Venkateswara (Balaji). The city is also known for its cultural heritage, educational institutions, and scenic surroundings.
